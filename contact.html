An Old Idea, Done Better Our Story

Our founder, Andrew Ziegenhorn, was a college student where he first noticed a frequent inconvenience: he hated how hard it was to find good clothes! With that in mind he founded ThreadMatch, a modern solution to a classic problem, bringing many clothing brands of all kinds under one roof to streamline your shopping process.

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee with every product sold, because at the end of the day what we care about is you; and making your world a more efficient, beautiful, and simple place.
